Angles!

To get the ball in the pocket, you must imagine the angle it would take. By playing, you find out which number makes which angle. This should help you know your angles!

The numbers are:

  • 0° for straight up (North),
  • 90° for right (East),
  • 180° for down (South) and
  • 270° for left (West).

This way of measuring angles is called bearings and is used by orienteers,
ships and planes to find their way.

Score

The aim of the game is to pot the ball in as few attempts as possible. The user is encouraged to strategically plan their turns. Good luck.
